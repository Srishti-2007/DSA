Animal Species Information

Species Profile Picture Facts
Cat A cat is a small, soft, and playful animal. It likes to sleep for many hours in a day. Cats can see well in the dark and love to clean themselves by licking their fur.
Dog A dog is a very friendly and loyal animal. It loves to play with people and can smell things much better than humans. Dogs are known as man's best friend.
Elephant An elephant is the biggest land mammal. It has big ears, a long trunk, and strong legs. Elephants are very smart, gentle, and love to live in herds.
Rabbit A rabbit is a cute animal with long ears and soft fur. It likes to eat carrots and green leaves. Rabbits can jump very fast and are very active.
Lion A lion is called the king of the jungle. It is a strong and brave animal. Lions live in groups called prides, and the male lion has a big mane around its head.
